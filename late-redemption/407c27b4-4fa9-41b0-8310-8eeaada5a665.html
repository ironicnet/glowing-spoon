Description

Nongünz is a nihilistic action platformer which combines hectic shootouts with the management of a mysterious idle game.

Each run will have you parting from a black and white graveyard into an ever-changing gothic dungeon full of nightmares fleshed out of human viscera. Surviving this distraught roguelike will not only require skill but style, since Nongünz rewards you for long-lasting combos and challenges... moreover, score will play a key role in the progress of the idle game: the formation of your very own band of lost souls and death cultists.

As the world of Nongünz evolves, you will meet special characters that will inhabit your graveyard: praying cultists, self-scourging martyrs, somber gravediggers... which will either help you widen your killing skills or improve the performance of the insidious idle game built around the graveyard.

Features
  • Unforgiving gameplay that will challenge both your skill and your wits.
  • The whole game is a riddle. Unveil the mystery behind the nihilistic universe of Nongünz.
  • Beautifully crafted black and white pixel environments and characters.
  • Hundreds of levels plagued with dozens of differents enemies.
  • Many weapons, items and equipment to unlock and master.
  • The potential to change your perception about existence, violence and mankind.
