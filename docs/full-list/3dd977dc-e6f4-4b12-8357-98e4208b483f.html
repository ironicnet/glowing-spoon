Description

Incremental Epic Hero 2 is a reimagining of the original Incremental Epic Hero. A mixture of incremental/idle and roleplaying game set in a fantasy world filled with monsters and things to explore.

Real Time Combat

While playing as one of the six available characters, the battle continues on non-stop! Venture further into the world to encounter new monsters, better equipment, and complete quests to continually grow stronger.

World Map

There are whole new regions to explore and an expanded variety of monsters to face. Fight your way through armies of slimes, spiders, foxes and more! Each region grows more difficult than the last, and with the power to prestige the areas you've already visited, you can keep all content relevant no matter where you are at in the game!


New Town System

Beginning with the Statue of Heroes, grow and expand the buildings in your town to unlock access to new features and expanded content!


Features

  • 6 characters each with their own set of upgradable skills that can even be shared and used by other characters
  • Nearly every monster in the game is capturable. Level them up to improve their unique special abilities
  • Craft dozens of different potions with Alchemy as well as dozens more various enchantment scrolls to power-up your equipment
  • Hundreds of various upgrades that allow you to progress harder and faster
  • Prestige your characters to gain powerful boosts to allow them to grow back even stronger
  • Prestige even the whole world with World Ascension to unlock even more content and new even more powerful boosts
  • Area and Dungeon Prestige to keep those older areas relevant and meaningful
  • Over an hundred different pieces of equipment to collect and improve
  • Challenges Bosses return along with an entirely new set of engaging set of challenge battles
  • Carefully crafted and balanced with the aid of our beloved Discord community
