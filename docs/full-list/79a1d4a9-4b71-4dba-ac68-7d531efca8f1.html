Description


Bounce around pinball-esque worlds controlling Rockhound, an interdimensional punk who loves cool sunglasses! Collect valuable Gems around Grand Palace, a place filled with doors, secrets, and friends.
Discover a new action-adventure game with 3D cinematics, live footage, funky tunes, and stylized pixel art! All crafted with love by Jupitron!

There are 3 worlds in Power Solenoid, which include:

  • 18 Levels with varying gravities, dangers, collectibles, and secrets (some of them feature friends with rewards)
  • 7 boss fights with unique patterns to uncover and master, divided into MiniBosses, BigBosses, and the epic Final Battle with [*********]
  • 6 Secret levels with increased difficulty!
  • 3 Special reward bonus levels (hidden)
  • A soundtrack with more than 40 mind-bending loop songs for every moment!

A lot of Cosmic Items!

  • Use your Astral Mirror to equip your new face, Cosmic Glasses, and Cosmic Tools!
  • 6 Cosmic Glasses that Rockhound can receive, buy, and discover throughout Grand Palace.
  • 3 Cosmic Tools that will aid Rockhound in navigating levels more easily and finding secret paths.

Special stages and Minigames!

  • Discover your amazing Starship called GlideHawk; use it to navigate classic-style shoot-em-up levels.
  • 2 friendly Goblins that will ask you for unusual Earthly items they're curious about.
  • Goblins that work in Grand Palace! Bankula runs an Exchange Gems business and Lion dreams of being a millionaire; you can help him achieve his dreams.
  • Rockhound can find minigames! Rockbowl is a Golden Coin farm that you can level up to obtain this strange, valuable earthly metal!
  • Besides Goblins, in Grand Palace, you can find Benny, a charming plasticine little dude who aspires to reach the heights of the universe! But he needs your help!

Secrets and more secrets!

  • Uncover the mysterious tomb of someone called Herman. Can you find the strange parts missing from the Tomb?
  • In Grand Palace, there are days! Goblins will sell different kinds of things each day; perhaps some days they're unavailable, who knows where they are?
  • Goblins will engage in a friendly competition called Smog, a reflex mind battle that will test your sight.
  • Goblins can evolve! Alter the appearance of a Goblin by strengthening your bond with them.
  • Goblins are collectors! They will sell you their valued items; these items will aid Rockhound in his journey.
  • Grand Palace isn’t exempt from the system! Be cautious with your Cosmic Card expenses, a Credit Card from the outer dimensions!



Embark on this cosmic journey and join Rockhound in his quest through Power Solenoid!
